Mom & Me Scarf

Was created for the new mom to be able to enjoy the first time with their new born and to enable moms to walk into any situation knowing that in an instant they can create their own private space for the baby. 

Nursing an infant can prove challenging when in public areas ! Apron style nursing covers are plentiful on the market.  We designed our scarf differently with not only function but with fashion in mind. Our scarf is a fashion accessory that is used during the nursing period. The scarf is worn instead of carried in the diaper bag and also remains a staple item in your wardrobe for years to come.

We made upcycling easy….once nursing your infant is a sweet memory, your scarf can still be used as a fashion accessory.
