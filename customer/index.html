Return Policy

Returns and exchanges are simple and free For all Mom & Me Scarf purchases, we will gladly ex change or return your item(s) within 30 days of your purchase. The item(s) must be in new, unused condition with the original tags and/or packaging and be accompanied by a Return Authorization Number.

To request a Return Authorization Number please email with your full name and order number. Returns & Exchanges: returns@momandmescarf.com

Please allow 1-2 full business days to receive your return authorization number and further instructions. RETURNS ARE NOT AVAILBLE ON USED PRODUCT OR WITHOUT PACKAGING AND ORIGINAL TAGS: Any item deemed "used" will be ineligible for return. Pending inspection it is Mom & Me Scarf’s sole discretion to determine if the item(s) have been used or been damaged other than original manufacturing defects. If the items you returned are used, they will be returned to you and no return provided.
