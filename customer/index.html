Shipping Info

We ship Monday through Friday (excluding holidays) via UPS We ask for 1-2 days to process your order before it ships. Depending on where your package will be delivered, delivery dates will vary.

We currently offer FREE Ground Shipping on all orders (Continental U.S. only) over $70. Click here for international orders. Any other international customers please contact us for shipment arrangements and quotes. Ground shipping arrives within 2-6 days.

You can also choose upgraded shipping. We offer the following shipping options: We are more than happy to expedite your order at the following rates:
UPS 2-Day Air Shipping: $14
UPS Standard Overnight: $22

PLEASE NOTE: We can not be responsible for delivery delays due to unforeseen circumstances outside our reasonable control such as delays due to severe weather, natural disasters or strikes. If your shipment requires special attention, we are more than happy to try our best to accommodate your special requests. Please contact our Customer Service Department with your needs !

Please note: All orders with upgraded shipping must be placed before 11am PST in order to ship same day.

Shipping to a Post Office (PO) Box

We do not offer shipping to PO Boxes at this time. Please use your actual address for shipping. Any orders entered with a PO Box shipping address will delay processing.

Address Corrections / Undeliverable Addresses

Costs associated with incorrect addresses are charged by UPS and passed along to you, the customer

Address corrections can be made, free of charge, before your order has shipped by contacting our Customer Service Department at customerservice@momandmescarf.com. Orders already shipped requiring a change of address or a correction will incur a $17 intercept fee + an additional $7 shipping charge if the updated address is outside of the original ZIP code entered at the time of shipment. In the event an order cannot be delivered and is returned, a $7 return shipping fee is charged. Upon receipt of the returned shipment you have the following options available:

We are happy to reship the item for an additional $7 via UPS Ground. Since the package incurred a return shipping fee of $7, this option is $14. Please let us know if we need to update the shipping destination at this time.

We can simply return the item to stock and refund your card the product cost minus the incurred return shipping fee of $7.

Please contact us if you have any shipping and delivery questions or if you would like an estimated date of arrival for your package.

Return Policy

Returns and exchanges are simple and free For all Mom & Me Scarf purchases, we will gladly exchange or return your item(s) within 30 days of your purchase. The item(s) must be in new, unused condition with the original tags and/or packaging and be accompanied by a Return Authorization Number.

To request a Return Authorization Number please email with your full name and order number. Returns & Exchanges: customerservice@momandmescarf.com

Please allow 1-2 full business days to receive your return authorization number and further instructions.

RETURNS ARE NOT AVAILABLE ON USED PRODUCT OR WITHOUT PACKAGING AND ORIGINAL TAGS: Any item deemed "used" will be ineligible for return. Pending inspection it is Mom & Me Scarf’s sole discretion to determine if the item(s) have been used or been damaged other than original manufacturing defects. If the items you returned are used, they will be returned to you and no return provided.

YOUR REFUND will be processed within 5-7 business days upon receipt and inspection of your return. It will be refunded in the original form of payment. Please note we have no control over your banking institution and your banking institution may require additional days to process and post the transaction to your account.

Once you receive a return authorization number, please send your return with this number to:
Mom & Me Scarf
Nolte Design Company
Attn: Returns - RA#
PO Box 540 383
Orlando Florida 32854

For your protection, we recommend you return all items via a traceable method from a carrier such as USPS, UPS or FedEx. NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY is not responsible for packages not received if a tracking number is not provided. Returns and exchanges must be shipped prepaid; we cannot accept C.O.D. deliveries.

Privacy Policy

NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY is committed to privacy.

NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY and our brands, (which include but are not limited to MOM & ME SCARF), value our customers and respect their privacy. We collect customer information in our store and through our websites in an effort to give you quality customer service, improve your shopping experience and communicate about our products, promotions, special events,sales and contests. As described in this policy, your information may be shared among our brands and, under certain circumstances, with third parties we may have partnered with in promotions or contests.

This policy describes the personal information we collect about our customers, how we use it and when we share it with third parties. Our policy also describes the choices our customers can make about how we collect and use their information. If our policy changes, we will post an update on our website.

What kind of information does NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY collect?

We may collect personal information from you, (such as your name, email and postal addresses, phone number, gender, birthday, etc.), when you save your information with us, make a purchase, correspond with us or when you participate in a contest, promotion or survey. We use this information to help personalize your shopping experience and provide advertising best tailored to your interests.

We also monitor use of the website and traffic patterns in order to improve website design and the products and services we offer, as well as to determine what offers, promotions or information to send to you.

Does NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY share my information?

NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY does not sell customer contact information, though we may disclose information about you to other companies we’ve teamed up with for specific promotions and as required by law, governmental request or court order, if based on our good faith belief that it is necessary to conform or comply with such law or request.

Our Email List

By signing up for our mailing list, you may receive notice of sales, promotions and new merchandise, as well as company news and special offers. Email addresses may be shared with other members of the NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY corporate family. If you would like to unsubscribe from our email list, please contact us (see below on how to contact us). We will remove your name from our list as soon as we can, but please recognize that you may receive another email before we are able to remove you.

Secure Shopping

For your security when placing online orders, we use industry-standard SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption when transferring and receiving customer data exchanged through our site server. SSL is a technology that vendors use to ensure the security of online business. To purchase from the NOLTE DESIGN COMPANY online boutique, you must use an SSL-enabled browser such as Mozilla or Explorer. This protects the confidentiality of your personal and credit card information while it is transmitted over the Internet. All other data transfer is conducted through a secure phone line.

If you have questions about our privacy policy, we invite you to contact us.
