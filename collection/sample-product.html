Feather light cotton voile creme nursing scarf

$59.00

The Viscose fabric drapes beautifully around your neck as a scarf and is very light and airy. Get a bit of luxury during the first time with your newborn. The Viscose fabric has been designed with this purpose in mind and is soft to the touch and will give you privacy while keeping air flowing through fabrics to naturally keep temperature lower during feeding.

Add to Cart
Product Features
  • Patent pending nursing scarf with a stabilizing edge design that allows for mom and baby to keep eye contact while nursing.
  • Viscose - for cool comfort, soft while delicately sheltering baby while nursing.
  • Adjustable buttons assure a flawless fit.
  • Perfectly sized at 21" x 76" (approximate measurements).
  • Can of course also be worn as a cover during nursing.
How to use
  • Wear your scarf as a regular accessory until you need to feed, undo the scarf and locate the buttons on the upper edge of the scarf, there are two height selections for your convenience! Snap a button on each side and drape the fabric behind your back or to the front.
  • The stabilizing edge creates a viewing area for mom and baby to keep eye contact while getting privacy. Once your child is eating solid foods, you can easily remove the stabilizing edge and use scarf as a regular accessory for years to come!
Care Instructions
  • Although made in thin and delicate fabric this is a machine washable scarf!
  • Treat it gently as you would any finer scarf.
  • Remove the stabilizing edge before wash.
  • Machine wash cold.
  • Wash separatley as colors may bleed slightly.
  • Gentle cycle recommended.
  • Do not bleach.
  • Do not tumble dry & do not dry in direct sunlight.
  • Low iron when needed.
  • Light shrinkage may occur.
